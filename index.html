Our History

Eagle Signs began its existence when another great business closed its doors. Over the decades Pelican Sign Services, Inc had built up a team with a wealth of experience and a variety of expertise, and a reputation for quality products and reliable service. When the owner of Pelican Signs decided to close the business, a few of the employees made an offer to buy the equipment so that they may start their own company, staffing it with the same people that had made Pelican Signs so great. He agreed, and Eagle Signs and Designs, LLC was born.

Our Future

Eagle Signs and Designs, LLC has taken to heart the good practices and lessons learned from Pelican Sign Services, Inc. We are working to uphold the quality of our products, which Pelican Signs was so well known for. We also strive to improve on our internal processes, in order to provide better service to our clients, and a beneficial environment for our employees. We hope to bring value to our community by offering a robust variety of products and services to meet your needs.

Our Process

From start to finish, we want to make sure the product you recieve meets or exceeds your expectations. We make an effort to maintain an open communication so that we can be certain that we are meeting your needs. Once you have seen and approved our artwork, we procede to use high quality materials, so that the product will stand up to weather, graffiti, and time. Our products are meant to last years, saving you money over time.

Products and Services

Eagle Signs provides a variety of products and services to meet your needs. Whether your goal is to improve your brand, sell a house, or identify office suites, we have a variety of high quality solutions. Not sure what you need for your project? Our friendly sales team can help you find the best solution with a FREE consultation.

Graphic Design

Let us help you build your brand. Our talented artists can help you design a new logo, or update an old one.

Real Estate

Yard signs, open house A-frames, sold riders, and more. Our products are made with high quality materials, meant to last, and easy to clean.

ADA and Engraving

Tactile and braille signs identifying exit routes, restrooms, conference rooms, safety hazards, and more. We can fulfill your needs, so you can pass your inspection. Want something engraved? We have a variety of materials on which you can leave your mark.

Monuments

If you want your business to be visible from the street, we can create a solid or backlit sign that will draw attention to your locale.

Vehicle Graphics

Advertise your business on the go with logos and contact info applied directly to your vehicle. Or on an easy to remove magnet.

Directories and Suite Plaques

Help people find you in multi-tenant buildings. We can create new directories and wayfinding programs, or update existing ones. A unique suite plaque will help comunicate your location to clients.

Impact Logos

One of the best ways to leave a lasting impression is to have your company identity prominantly displayed so that it's the first thing people see when they enter your building.

Miscellaneous

Need to communicate health and safety guidlines? Evacuation plans? Parking or delivery instructions? Event Maps? Name Badges? Stickers! And much more. We've got you covered.
